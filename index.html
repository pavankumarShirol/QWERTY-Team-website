About Us

QWERTY.IO was started in January 2019 by a group of dedicated and passionate students who wanted to help their peers to grow along with themselves. It was started with the aim to inculcate industry level knowledge on top of the curriculum and bring about the much needed awareness among the students.

QWERTY.IO conducts the workshop often which helps the students to have a hands-on session with interactive instructions and helpers on the various topics. The students were able to get an overview of the technologies and benefits.

Workshops

QWERTY.I/O conducts Workshops that provide participants with a focused opportunity to learn new technical skills and knowledge.

Participants can get hands-on experience with new tools, technologies, or processes so that they can kick-start their tech journey. This can help them to become more effective and efficient in their work, and to take on new challenges and responsibilities as the team believes Technical workshops can stimulate innovation and creativity by exposing participants to new ideas and approaches.

  • Learn new skills and knowledge.
  • Practice and apply what they learn.
  • Interact and collaborate with industry relevant people.
  • Professional as well as personal development.
  • Innovation and creativity.
